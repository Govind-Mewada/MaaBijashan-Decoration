Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our decoration services

How far in advance should I book your decoration services?

We recommend booking our services at least 2-3 months in advance, especially for peak wedding seasons. However, we also accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability.

Do you provide decoration services outside your city?

Yes, we provide decoration services across various locations. Additional travel and accommodation charges may apply for venues outside our city.

Can I customize the decoration packages according to my budget?

Absolutely! We understand that every client has different needs and budgets. We offer customized decoration solutions to fit your specific requirements and budget constraints.

Do you offer venue visits before the event?

Yes, we conduct venue visits to understand the space better and plan the decoration accordingly. This helps us create a more accurate and suitable decoration plan for your event.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies based on the timing of cancellation. Generally, cancellations made 30 days before the event date are eligible for a partial refund. Please contact us for more specific details.
